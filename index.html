Welcome to Kimiad

Discover a picturesque golf destination set amongst rolling terrain and free-roaming animals. With an 18-hole course, executive course, par 3 course, driving range, walking trails, and on-site restaurants, Kimiad offers something for golfers and nature lovers of every level.

⛳

Multiple Courses

18-hole course, 12-hole executive course, and a 6-hole par 3 — something for every skill level.

🏌️

Driving Range

Warm up or work on your swing at our fully equipped driving range and chip & putt area.

🌿

Trails & Nature

Walking and hiking trails through beautiful scenery with free-roaming animals throughout the estate.

🍽️

Restaurants & Bar

Relax after your round at one of our on-site restaurants with great food, drinks, and views.

Green Fees

Affordable rates for an exceptional golfing experience. No tee time required — just pitch up and play.
